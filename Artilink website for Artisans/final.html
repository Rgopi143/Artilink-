Platform Features

For Artisans

Direct Market Access – Sell handcrafted products directly to businesses without middlemen.
Fair Trade & Transparent Pricing – Ensure artisans receive fair compensation for their craftsmanship.
Easy Product Listing – Simple interface to upload products with descriptions and images.
Secure Payments – Guaranteed payments through secure transactions.
Product Authentication – Verify and certify the authenticity of handmade goods.
AI-Powered Insights – Get data-driven recommendations on pricing and demand trends.
Seamless Logistics Support – Integrated shipping solutions for hassle-free deliveries.
Community & Training – Access to skill development programs and artisan communities.

For Businesses

Curated Handmade Products – Access to a wide range of authentic handcrafted goods.
Direct B2B Purchasing – Buy directly from artisans, reducing costs and ensuring originality.
Bulk Order Management – Simplified bulk ordering process for large-scale procurement.
Fair Trade Certification – Ensure ethical sourcing and sustainability in purchases.
AI-Based Matching – Smart recommendations for artisans based on business needs.
Verified Artisan Profiles – Trustworthy sourcing with background-verified artisans.
Secure Transactions & Contracts – Transparent and safe payment systems.
Customized Orders – Ability to request custom-made products from skilled artisans.
